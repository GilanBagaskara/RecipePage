Ayam Rarang

Lombok's culinary specialties are famous for their spicy taste. One of them is chicken rarang which is cooked with spicy red spices.

The name rarang chicken itself is taken from an area called Rarang in the East Lombok region.

There is a place to eat chicken rarang which is famous for its deliciousness. The name is Rarang Inaq Dellah Restaurant. This very simple restaurant has been established since 1976. Until now, it has reached 45 years of age.

Resources that can help to make dishes (Food Recipe)

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of free-range chicken, cut according to taste
  • tamarind water
  • brown sugar
  • powdered pepper to taste
  • salt to taste
  • lime

    • Ground spices:

  • 10 bird's eye chilies
  • 10 dried chilies
  • 10 large red chilies
  • 7 cloves of garlic
  • 3 roasted hazelnuts
  • 1/2 tsp shrimp paste

Steps Making Ayam Taliwang

  1. Flush the chicken that has been cut with lime juice. Leave it for about 10 minutes. After that, wash the chicken under running water, drain.
  2. Prepare a teflon or frying pan, then heat it. Add the chicken, then fry briefly until half cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. Heat enough cooking oil, then sauté the ground spices until fragrant and cooked.
  4. Add the chicken, then stir well. Add sugar, salt, ground pepper, and tamarind water solution. Stir again and cook until the chicken is cooked.
  5. Serve chicken rarang with warm rice.

Note

  1. Don't grind the seasoning for too long because the characteristics of the seasoning are a bit rough.
  2. Sauté the ground spices until fragrant and cooked so they are not unpleasant.
  3. If the chicken is still a little tough, you can add water and cook it until the water reduces.
