Plecing Kangkung

Tired of stir-fried kale? Now you can be creative by making Lombok's typical kangkung plecing. Usually the main ingredient for making this dish uses Lombok's typical kale which has a large stem characteristic.

Even so, you can still make plecing kangkung using ordinary kale that is sold in the market.

The unique thing about this plecing kangkung is the serving process, where the boiled and cooked kale is not served hot and you have to wait until the kale is at normal room temperature. If it's cool, kangkung is served with a special chili sauce on top.

Resources that can help to make dishes (RasaBunda)

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch (300g) kale
  • 100g bean sprouts, brew hot water until wilted

    • Chilli Sauce:

  • 10 red bird's eye chilies
  • 3 curly red chilies
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of grilled shrimp paste
  • 1 tsp salt

    • Comprementary:

  • lime
  • fried peanuts

Steps Making Plecing Kangkung

  1. Cuci bersih kangkung lalu rendam dengan air garam selama 10 menit lalu tiriskan.
  2. Didihkan air secukupnya, beri garam lalu rebus kangkung sebentar saja kira-kira selama 5 menit, tiriskan.
  3. Rebus dahulu bawang merah, bawang putih, cabe dan tomat kira-kira selama 5 menit, tiriskan.
  4. Ulek kasar semua bahan bumbu sambal, lalu tambahkan terasi, garam, gula merah dan kaldu jamur. Koreksi rasa, jika sudah pas kucuri air jeruk nipis aduk hingga rata.
  5. Tata kangkung di piring sajian lalu tuang sambal diatasnya kemudian taburkan kacang tanah goreng.
  6. Plecing kangkung siap disantap.

Note

When boiling the kale, add the kale when the water is really boiling and don't boil it for too long. To keep kale green and crunchy.
