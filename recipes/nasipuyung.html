Nasi Balap Puyung

Spicy seasoning is one of the culinary characteristics on the island of Lombok. One of the famous culinary specialties that makes many people want to taste it is Nasi Balap Puyung, a dish consisting of white rice, shredded spicy chicken, dried shredded chicken, plus fried peanuts and soybeans. A simple culinary dish, looks so simple, but with extraordinary taste.

A rice with side dishes of shredded chicken and soybeans. There are many versions and references to why this Lombok culinary is called racing rice, including according to some community leaders, because at first this rice used to be sold around using bicycles, where the buyer sometimes had to chase the seller who likes speeding on his bicycle when selling.

Resources that can help to make dishes (Food Recipe)

Ingredients

  • Rice, portion can be adjusted

    • Shredded Chicken Ingredients:

  • 500 grams of chicken breast that has been boiled and shredded
  • 2 stalks of lemon grass that has been crushed
  • Galangal with the size of 1 knuckles, then crushed
  • 1 lime
  • 7 sticks of red chilies
  • 9 sticks of cayenne pepper, the amount of chili can be changed according to taste
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 7 shallots
  • 5 hazelnuts that have been roasted
  • Kencur for 1 knuckles
  • Ginger for 1 knuckles
  • Terasi, dose according to taste
  • Salt and sugar, to taste

    • Dried Potato Ingredients:

  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp whiting

    • Peanut Saute Ingredients:

  • Long beans that have been sliced lengthwise, the amount as needed
  • 1 clove of garlic that has been chopped
  • Salt or seasoning according to taste

    • Fried Soybean Ingredients:

  • 100 grams of soybeans, may use packaged soybeans

Steps Making Nasi Balap Puyung

    Making Ayam Suir

  1. Blend the spices that have been prepared for the shredded chicken except lemon grass, lime and galangal
  2. Saute ground spices with a little oil, wait until fragrant
  3. Add lemongrass and galangal, add half a cup of boiled water or broth from the remaining chicken boiled water
  4. Cook until the spices are cooked, then add the shredded chicken breast
  5. Cook until the chicken starts to dry out then add the lime
  6. Fry a little, then transfer to a container

    Make Dried Potatoes:

  1. Grate the potatoes, either coarse or fine grated
  2. Soak the potatoes in water mixed with whiting and salt for 1 hour
  3. Drain, then wash the potatoes thoroughly
  4. Fry the potatoes until crisp, use a large amount of oil so that they are cooked evenly
  5. Once dry and golden brown, drain

    How to Make Sauteed Peanuts:

  1. Saute garlic until fragrant with a little oil
  2. Enter the long beans that have been sliced
  3. Add salt or seasoning to taste
  4. Transfer to a container when it is cooked

    Making Fried Soybeans:

  1. Soak soybeans in salted water for about 30 minutes
  2. Fry until cooked, then drain

Note

For serving, prepare the rice on a plate then place one by one the complementary menu on the side of the rice side by side. Serve with fresh vegetables, and Nasi Balap Puyung is ready to be served!
