Sayur Ares

Sayur ares is a typical food of Lombok Island with the main raw material being young banana fronds or kedebong. These vegetables are cooked with coconut milk and a few pieces of beef which will later be seasoned with natural spices. However, some areas also make this food without beef.

With unique raw materials cooked with coconut milk and various spices, of course this food has a distinctive taste. The taste of this vegetable is very unique, which is sweet and savory.

Resources that can help to make dishes (Food Recipe)

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of young banana stems
  • 1 kg of duck or chicken bones
  • 5 grams of galangal
  • 10 grams of tamarind
  • 10 grams of lemon grass
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Coconut oil to taste

    • Ground spices

  • 75 grams of shallots
  • 30 grams of garlic
  • 15 grams of red chilies
  • 5 grams of turmeric
  • 5 grams of shrimp paste
  • 5 grams of ginger
  • 10 grams of hazelnut
  • Salt to taste

Steps Making Sayur Ares

  1. Thinly slice the banana tree trunk. Add oil, salt and acid. Squeeze the banana tree trunk until limp.
  2. Saute ground spices. Add lemongrass, bay leaf and galangal. Cook until fragrant. Remove and set aside.
  3. Boil meat and bones until tender. Add banana leaves and ground spices. Boil until done. Serve the ares jukut while warm.
