Sambal Beberuk

Those who have tried Taliwang chicken will know how delicious this chili made from fresh eggplant is. The spicy taste and the fresh combination of eggplant make this Beberuk Sambal different from the others.

Even though this typical Lombok chili sauce is usually eaten with Taliwang chicken, you can really eat it with other dishes. Especially those who like fresh things, you will definitely like this chili sauce.

Resources that can help to make dishes (Food Recipe)

Ingredients

  • 10 long bean sticks, cut into 2 cm pieces
  • 3 round purple eggplants
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

    • Ground spices:

  • 2 bird's eye chilies
  • 3 curly red chilies
  • 4 red onions
    • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp grilled shrimp paste
  • 1 tsp sugar 1 cm kencur
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps Making Sambal Beberuk

  1. Cut the purple eggplant into cubes or adjust to taste. Cut the tomatoes into 8 parts.
  2. Place the eggplant and tomatoes on a serving plate with the long bean pieces.
  3. Mix the ground spices with hot cooking oil. Pour over the vegetables, add the lime juice on top.
